How do I use mass order in SMMHub?

To utilize the Mass Order feature, enter each line with the service ID, a pipe symbol (|), the URL, another pipe symbol, and the quantity. Find the service ID on our service list. For example, to add Instagram Followers to three accounts (abcd, asdf, qwer), and if the service ID for "Instagram Followers [10K] [REAL] ⚡️💧⭐" is 100, you would format it like this: 100|https://demo.com|1000, 101|https://demo2.com/|1000, 102|https://demo3.com/|1000.

How do I get a YouTube comment link?

Find the timestamp next to your username above the comment (e.g., "3 days ago"). Hover over it, right-click, and select "Copy Link Address." The URL will look like https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=12345&lc=a1b21etc instead of https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=54321. Paste this URL into your browser’s address bar to confirm the comment appears as a "Highlighted comment."
